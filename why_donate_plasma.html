What is Plasma?

Plasma is basically the liquid part of your blood. Function of plasma is to keep the blood healthy. It carries proteins, minerals, nutrients and hormones to designated places in your body. It contains salts and enzymes which help create anti-bodies which further fight the infection or virus in the body. In Covid it helps the patients fight against the virus through the anti-bodies present in plasma.


How Does Plasma Donation Help In Covid?

According to experts, Plasma is not a cure to Covid but helps fight it and eradicate the virus cells present in the body. When a patient is suffering from Covid-19, her ability to fight the virus decreases due to which the person is unable to recover himself. Plasma donation helps to provide anti-bodies to the patient which assists the cells to overcome the corona virus cells.

Person who has recovered from Covid 14 days prior can donate his plasma as his body must have formed anti-bodies against the virus. Plasma maintains normal blood pressure and blood volume levels in the body which is crucial for a person suffering from Corona.


You Can Donate Plasma for COVID-19 If :

  • You are between 18 and 65 years
  • Tested negative for the virus at least 14 days before the plasma can be used
  • Were asymptomatic at the time of contracting the disease
  • Not suffering from any transmissible disease
  • Women with no kids
